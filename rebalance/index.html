Rebalance Switches

Click Menu and then select the Rebalance Switches option and click ‘OK’.

The following screen is displayed:

Rebalance Switches Screen

This screen is used to display any rebalance investment switches that may have been made, or are in the process of being made for this member.

There are separate screens for each investment switch a member has made. To navigate between the investment switches, click ‘ Previous’ or ‘ Next’.

1. Basic Member Switch Details

This is the screen shown above and displays basic details about the switch.

2. Member Switch Details

This screen displays details of which fund(s) to switch out of and which fund(s) to switch into. It also shows the proportions to be switched in/out.

Further details of how investment switches are done in Pension Master can be found in Investment Switches.

To add a switch for the member, click ‘Add’.

To enter sale details for an existing switch, click ‘Update’.

To process a sale of units for a switch that is marked as Pending, click ‘Sell’.

To delete an existing switch, click ‘Delete’. You will not be able to delete an existing switch if:

  • the entry on Investment History relating to the switch in have been posted to units.
  • there are any switches for this member after date requested for the switch to be deleted.
