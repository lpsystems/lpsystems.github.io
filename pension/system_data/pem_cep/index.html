Introduction

Only users set up as Supervisor status in Users may access this program. The type of access granted can be view only or full access. For the view only option, you will not be permitted access to any menu optionn that allows you to add/update/delete data. If you do not have Supervisor status, you will not be permitted access to this program.

This program allows maintenance of the CEP factors that are issued by the UK government and are used in certain statutory calculations to do with contracting out of SERPS in the UK.

The following options and screen appear when this program is selected:

  • Update
  • Report
  • Browse
  • Exit

CEP Factors Screen

Add A CEP Factors

Click ‘Update’.

Click on ‘Add Row’ to add a blank line ready for input of the new months CEP details.

APRIL 5TH - Enter the year for your CEP Factors in the format yyyy. This is to be the year in which the 5th April in that tax year falls e.g. for the 92/93 tax year enter 1993 as the 5th April in the 92/93 tax year is 5/4/93.

DIVISOR FACTOR - Enter the CEP Divisor Factor for the tax year.

CEP FACTOR - Enter the CEP Factor for the tax year. This is the total (employees plus employers) NI contribution reduction as a result of the member contracting out of SERPS.

CA FACTOR - Enter the CA (Certified Amount) Factor for the tax year. This is the employees NI contribution reduction as a result of the member contracting out of SERPS.

When the CEP details have been entered click on ‘OK’ to save the details to file. You can click on ‘Cancel’ at any stage to exit the add option without saving the entered CEP details.

Browsing CEP Details

Click on the ‘Browse’ button or the Browse icon on the toolbar to view details in any screen where the data does not fit on the screen.

Once in browse mode, use the arrow keys or the scrollbar on the right of the data to navigate around the data.

Click on OK or Cancel to leave browse mode.

Printing CEP Factor Details

The options for printing are described in the document Printing From Pension Master.

Exit Program

Click ‘Exit’. This leaves the program and returns to the System Data menu.
