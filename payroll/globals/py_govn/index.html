Introduction

This program allows maintenance of the UK/Scottish Income Tax Rates details. This is where you will find data relating to annual statistics, including tax bands and tax rates. The annual statistics can be added and updated in this program.

The UK and Scottish tax rates must both be set up here for the tax year, before you attempt to run the Payroll Run program.

The following options and screens appear when this program is selected.

  • Add
  • Query
  • Update
  • Report
  • Exit

Query/Search For Income Tax Rates Details

Click ‘Query’. The following pop-up window is displayed:

Query Screen

You may select records by using the Tax Year From field. Once you have entered your selection criteria, click ‘OK’ to proceed and retrieve the records from the database or click ‘Cancel’ to abort the selection and return to the UK/Scottish Income Tax Rates menu.

When a record is found to match your selection criteria, it is displayed to the screen.

Adding UK/Scottish Income Tax Rates Details

Click ‘Add’.

The UK/Scottish Income Tax Rates screens are used to hold the annual statistics, which are produced each year by the Government. Ensure you are viewing the UK Income Tax Rates screen on clicking ‘Add’ if it’s UK Income Tax Rates you are wanting to add. Ensure you are viewing the Scottish Income Tax Rates screen on clicking ‘Add’ if it’s Scotish Income Tax Rates you are wanting to add.

Enter the fields as required as follows:

TAX YEAR FROM - Enter the year the tax year starts e.g. if the tax year is 2017/2018, then you enter 2017 in this field.

TAX YEAR TO - This field is automatically calculated by Payroll Master e.g. if 2017 is entered in the tax year from field, then the tax year to field is calculated as 2018. You cannot input in this field.

NUMBER OF BANDS - Enter the number of tax bands for the tax year. The number entered in this field will determine the number of rows that must be entered in the table below.

OVERRIDING REGULATORY LIMIT % - Enter the overriding regulatory % to be used for all tax codes.

BAND WIDTH - Enter the width of the tax band.

FROM - The amount for this field is automatically calculated by Payroll Master from the amount entered in the band width field. This field is not accessible to the user.

TO - The amount of this field is automatically calculated by Payroll Master from the amount entered in the band width field. This field is not accessible to the user.

RATE - Enter the % rate at which the amount of pay in the band is to be calculated at.

BASIC RATE BAND - Tick this field if the tax band is classed as the Basic Rate Band.

Click ‘OK’ to save the UK/Scottish Income Tax Rates Details, or click ‘Cancel’ to exit the screen without saving the details entered.

Updating UK/Scottish Income Tax Rates Details

The fields listed above can be updated, apart from the ‘Tax Year From’ and ‘Tax Year To’ fields, and the fields which aren’t accessible in the table.

Browsing UK/Scottish Income Tax Rates Details

Click ‘Browse’ to view the data that does not fit on the screen.

In browse mode, use the scrollbar on the right of the screen to navigate through the data.

Click ‘OK’ or ‘Cancel’ to leave browse mode.

Viewing UK/Scottish Income Tax Rates Details

To select all tax rate settings, click ‘Query’ and leave the ‘Tax Year From’ field blank. Then click ‘OK’. To view these other records selected, click on Next to view the next record, and Previous to view the previous record. Click the UK and Scottish screen buttons to flick between the 2 types of Income Tax Rates.

You can also use the options on the toolbar to view the first, previous, next and last records in your query.

Printing UK/Scottish Income Tax Rates Details

Click the ‘Report’ icon.

If Income Tax Rates have been selected then the following window appears:

Select Report Options Window

The options are as follows:

Current record Produce a report for the tax year details currently displayed to the screen.
All selected record(s) Produce a report for all selected tax year details.
Select record(s) to print A window appears, which allows you to make a selection.

If legislation details have not been selected then the following window appears:

Select Report Options Window

You can select the record(s) to print in this dialog box. Further details of the printing options can be found here.

Select the appropriate option and click ‘OK’. Click ‘Cancel’ to exit the printing or ‘Help’ to display this help text.

Exit Program

Click ‘Exit’. This leaves the program and closes the current browser tab.
