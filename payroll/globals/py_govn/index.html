Introduction

This program allows maintenance of the Government Legislation details. This is where you will find data relating to annual statistics, including tax bands and tax rates. The annual statistics can be added and updated in this program.

The government legislation details must be set up here for the tax year, before you attempt to run the Payroll Run program.

The following options and screen appear when this program is selected.

  • Add
  • Query
  • Update
  • Report
  • Exit

Legislation Settings Screen

Query/Search For Legislation Details

Click ‘Query’. The following pop-up window is displayed:

Query Screen

You may select records by using the Tax Year From field. Once you have entered your selection criteria, click ‘OK’ to proceed and retrieve the records from the database or click ‘Cancel’ to abort the selection and return to the Government Legislation menu.

When a record is found to match your selection criteria, it is displayed to the screen.

Adding Government Legislation Details

Legislation Settings

Click ‘Add’.

The Legislation Settings screen is used to hold the annual statistics, which are produced each year by the Government.

Enter the fields as required as follows:

TAX YEAR FROM - Enter the year the tax year starts e.g. if the tax year is 2000/2001, then you enter 2000 in this field.

TAX YEAR TO - This field is automatically calculated by Payroll Master e.g. if 2000 is entered in the tax year from field, then the tax year to field is calculated as 2001. You cannot input in this field.

NUMBER OF BANDS - Enter the number of tax bands for the tax year. The number entered in this field will determine the number of rows that must be entered in the table below.

‘K’ TAX CODES REGULATORY LIMIT % - Enter the regulatory % to be used for all prefix ‘K’ tax codes.

SCOTTISH VARIANCE - Enter the amount of Scottish variance to be used when calculating tax for Scottish tax codes.

BAND WIDTH - Enter the width of the tax band.

FROM - The amount for this field is calculated by Payroll Master from the amount entered in the band width field. You cannot input in this field.

TO - The amount of this field is calculated by Payroll Master from the amount entered in the band width field. You cannot input in this field.

RATE - Enter the % rate at which the amount of pay in the band is to be calculated at.

BASIC RATE BAND - Tick this field if the tax band is classed as the Basic Rate Band.

Click ‘OK’ to save the Government Legislation Details, or click ‘Cancel’ to exit the screen without saving the details entered.

Updating Government Legislation Details

The fields listed above can be updated, excluding the ‘Tax Year From’ and ‘Tax Year To’ fields, and the fields which cannot be accessed in the table.

Browsing Government Legislation Details

Click ‘Browse’ to view the data that does not fit on the screen.

In browse mode, use the scrollbar on the right of the screen to navigate through the data.

Click ‘OK’ or ‘Cancel’ to leave browse mode.

Viewing Government Legislation Details

To select all government legislation settings, click ‘Query’ and leave the ‘Tax Year From’ field blank. Then click ‘OK’. To view these other records selected, click on Next to view the next record, and Previous to view the previous record.

You can also use the options on the toolbar to view the first, previous, next and last records in your query.

Printing Government Legislation Details

Click the ‘Report’ icon.

If legislation details have been selected then the following window appears:

Select Report Options Window

The options are as follows:

Print current record Produce a report for the tax year details currently displayed to the screen.
Print all selected record(s) Produce a report for all selected tax year details.
Select record(s) to print A window appears, which allows you to make a selection.

If legislation details have not been selected then the following window appears:

Select Report Options Window

You can select the record(s) to print in this dialog box. Further details of the printing options can be found here.

Select the appropriate option and click ‘OK’. Click ‘Cancel’ to exit the printing or ‘Help’ to display this help text.

Exit Program

Click ‘Exit’. This leaves the program and closes the current browser tab.
